Curiosity: Textbook of Science for Grade 6

Book Overview & Pedagogy

Curricular Framework & Vision
  • NEP 2020 & NCF-SE 2023: Developed in alignment with the National Education Policy 2020 and the National Curriculum Framework for School Education 2023.
  • Competency-Based Education: Shifts focus from rote memorization to experiential, hands-on learning and critical thinking.
  • Pañchakośhas: Addresses the five planes of human existence to support holistic development during the Middle Stage (Grades 6 to 8).
  • Integrated Approach: Weaves together concepts from physics, chemistry, biology, and earth science alongside Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS) and environmental education.

Key Features & Structure
  • Non-Evaluative Elements: Includes sections like 'Do you know?', 'More to know!', 'Think it over!', 'More to do!', and 'Know a scientist' to spark curiosity without assessment pressure.
  • Chapter 1 (The Wonderful World of Science): Designed entirely as a non-evaluative, holistic introduction to scientific thinking.
  • ICT Integration: Features Quick Response (QR Codes) linking to digital resources such as videos, puzzles, and interactive quizzes.
