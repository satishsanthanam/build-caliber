Class 3 — Chapter 2: Badal and Moti

Story Summary: Badal and Moti

Plot Overview

The story is about a young boy named Badal who lives happily with his mother in a village. One cold evening, on his way home from school, Badal finds a shivering puppy and brings him home. With his mother's permission, they adopt the puppy and name him Moti. Badal and Moti become inseparable best friends, playing and eating together.

One rainy day, Badal slips and falls into a deep pit on his way home. When he doesn't return, his worried mother and the neighbours search for him. Moti uses his strong sense of smell to track Badal's scent, finds the pit, and barks to alert the search party. The neighbours rescue Badal using a rope, and a grateful Badal hugs and thanks his loyal dog.

Character Analysis

  • Badal: A kind-hearted, caring schoolboy who rescues a shivering puppy and loves his pet dearly.
  • Moti: A loyal, intelligent dog who grows up to be Badal's best friend and ultimately saves his life using his keen sense of smell.
  • Mother: A supportive and caring parent who allows Badal to keep the puppy on the condition that he takes responsibility for its care.
Grammar & Language Study

Past Tense Verbs (-ed ending)

The lesson introduces actions that have already happened in the past by adding -ed to base verbs:

  • lived (live + ed)
  • loved (love + ed)
  • played (play + ed)
  • gathered (gather + ed)
  • thanked (thank + ed)
  • walked (walk + ed)
  • talked (talk + ed)
  • asked (ask + ed)
  • touched (touch + ed)

Punctuation: Questions vs. Statements

Understanding when to use a question mark (?) versus a full stop (.):

  • Statements (use full stop): "The sun rises in the east.", "I like to eat mangoes.", "Brinda and Namrata are good friends."
  • Questions (use question mark): "What is your name?", "May I borrow your English textbook?", "Whose shoes are these?"
Exercises & Activities

Comprehension Questions

  • Q: What did Badal come across by the side of the road?
    A: A shivering puppy.
  • Q: What did Badal and his mother name the puppy?
    A: Moti.
  • Q: What did Badal fall into?
    A: A deep pit.
  • Q: How did Moti find Badal?
    A: Moti caught Badal's scent and followed it to the pit.

The Story of the Dove and the Ant

A classic fable completed using vocabulary words:

A dove was resting on a branch of a tree near a stream. An ant fell into the stream and cried for help. The dove dropped a leaf into the stream. The ant climbed on the leaf and landed safely. Another day the ant saw a hunter aiming at the dove. She quickly climbed up and bit the hunter's hand. The dove flew away unhurt.
