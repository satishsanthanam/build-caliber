Class 3 — Chapter 12: Chandrayaan

Chapter Summary

This chapter tells the story of a curious young girl named Rani who lives in a village and loves to ask questions about the world around her. After hearing her mother sing a traditional song about the Moon, Rani wonders how far away it is. The next morning, her brother Pratik excitedly shows her a TV broadcast announcing that India has landed on the Moon. Confused about how this was achieved, Rani and Pratik visit their neighbor, Aunt Nandini, who is a scientist.

Key Explanations by Aunt Nandini

  • Rocket: The vehicle used to travel from Earth to reach the Moon.
  • Chandrayaan: A Sanskrit-derived word where Chandra means Moon and Yaan means vehicle.
  • Important Dates: India launched Chandrayaan-3 on 14 July 2023, and it successfully landed on the Moon on 23 August 2023.
New Words & Vocabulary
  • curious: Eager to know or learn something.
  • excitement: A feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness.
  • machines: Apparatuses using mechanical power to perform tasks.
  • confused: Unable to think clearly or understand something.
  • scientist: A person who is studying or has expert knowledge of one or more of the natural or physical sciences.
  • rockets: Cylindrical projectiles used to travel into space.
  • Chandrayaan: India's lunar exploration mission vehicle.
Grammar & Language Study

Articles: 'a' and 'an'

We use articles before singular nouns based on their starting sounds:

  • Use 'a' before singular nouns that begin with consonant sounds (e.g., a girl, a village, a hanky, a dog, a sparrow).
  • Use 'an' before singular nouns that begin with vowel sounds (a, e, i, o, u) (e.g., an elephant, an eagle, an umbrella, an apricot).

Word Blends

Practice forming words using consonant blends:

  • sk: sky, skirt
  • sw: swings
  • sp: spoon, sparrow
  • st: story, stone

Ordinal Numbers & Days of the Week

Ordinal numbers indicate position in a sequence:

  • Sunday is the first day of the week.
  • Monday is the second day of the week.
  • Tuesday is the third day of the week.
  • Wednesday is the fourth day of the week.
  • Thursday is the fifth day of the week.
  • Friday is the sixth day of the week.
  • Saturday is the seventh day of the week.
Exercises & Activities

Comprehension Questions

  1. How do we know Rani was curious? (She asked many questions like "Why is the sky blue?" and "Where does the Sun go at night?")
  2. What did Pratik say in excitement? ("Look Rani, India is on the Moon!")
  3. Who was Nandini Aunty? (She was Vivaan's mother and a scientist.)
  4. What do the words Chandra and Yaan mean? (Chandra means Moon and Yaan means vehicle.)
  5. Complete the sentence: Chandrayaan-3 landed on the Moon on 23 August 2023.

Creative Thinking: 10 Days on the Moon

Imagine you are invited by scientists to spend 10 days on the moon. List items you would like to take with you (e.g., spacesuit, camera, water, oxygen cylinder, notebook, etc.).
