Class 2 — Chapter 5: Come Back Soon

Poem: Come Back Soon

Poem Recitation

Take a bus
Or take a train,
Take a boat
Or take a plane,

Take a taxi,
Take a car,
Maybe near
Or maybe far,

Take a rocket
To the moon,
But be sure
To come back soon.

Vocabulary & Sight Words

Sight Words

Practice reading these common words:

  • take
  • is
  • the
  • or
  • two
  • may be

New Words

Learn these new vocabulary words related to transport:

  • train
  • boat
  • plane
  • taxi
  • near
  • rocket
Let us Speak

Discussion Questions

Answer and discuss the following questions with your classmates:

  1. Have you ever travelled by a bus or a train?
  2. Where did you go?
  3. What did you eat on the way?
  4. What did you see from the window?
  5. Who else went with you?
  6. Did you have fun? Share it with the class.
Let us Listen

Action Poem

Listen to your teacher and read out this action poem:

Everybody stop,
Everybody stand,
Everybody sit,
and raise your hand.

Everybody jump,
Everybody run,
Everybody stop,
Oh! What fun.

Let us Write

Make a List: Means of Transport

Sort the different means of transport depending on the surface they move on:

  • Land: bus, car, train, jeep, bicycle, truck, cart, taxi, tractor
  • Air: aeroplane, helicopter
  • Water: ship, boat, yacht, raft
Phonics: Vowel Sound 'u'

Vowel Sound 'u' Words

Read the words below each category to practice the short vowel sound u (as in 'hut'):

  • Group 1: an umbrella, a truck, a duck
  • Group 2: a jug, a mug, a cup
  • Group 3: a bus, a rug, a hut

Grammar Note

Notice that the word an occurs before words beginning with the vowel letter 'u' (e.g., an umbrella), while a is used before consonant sounds.
