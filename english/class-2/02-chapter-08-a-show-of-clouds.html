Class 2 — Chapter 8: A Show of Clouds

Poem: A Show of Clouds

A delightful poem about a child looking up at the sky and imagining different shapes in the clouds.

Stanza 1: The White Bear

As I lie on my back, looking up at the sky. I see a big white bear, looking down at me.

Stanza 2: The Sailing Ship

As I lie on my back, looking up at the sky. I see a ship sailing, on a white sea.

Stanza 3: The Elephant

As I lie on my back, looking up at the sky. I see an elephant, waving its trunk at me.

Stanza 4: The Joy of Imagination

As I lie on my back, looking up at the sky. Oh! It’s fun, it’s fun to me, to see whatever I want to see.

Vocabulary & Word Study

New Words

  • lie: To rest flat on a surface.
  • bear: A large, heavy mammal with thick fur.
  • sea: A large body of salty water.
  • sailing: Moving smoothly across water.
  • waving: Moving to and fro.

Sight Words

Practice reading these words quickly: as, up, at, my, sky, back.

Activities & Exercises

Let us Speak

  • Cloud Watching: Sit outside and watch the clouds. Describe what shapes and colors you see (such as white, grey, black, red, blue).
  • Dialogue Practice:
    • Teacher: When you look up in the sky, what do you see?
      Child: When I look up, I see...
    • Teacher: When you look down at the ground, what do you see?
      Child: When I look down, I see...
    • Teacher: When you close your eyes, what do you see?
      Child: When I close my eyes, I see...

Let us Write

  1. Animals in the clouds: The child saw a bear and an elephant.
  2. Day Sky: Things seen during the day (such as the Sun, clouds, birds, airplanes).
  3. Night Sky: Things seen at night (such as the Moon and stars).

Let us Draw

Join the dots from Aa to Zz in alphabetical order to complete and color the butterfly picture.

Phonics: The 'ck' Sound

Repeat these words to practice the ck sound:

  • -ack: back, pack, lack, jack, rack
  • -ick: pick, tick, flick, thick
  • -uck: pluck, duck, muck, truck
